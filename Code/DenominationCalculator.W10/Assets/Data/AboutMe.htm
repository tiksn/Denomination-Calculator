This is a four section personal app to share with those you are close with who want keep up with you in one mobile application.

SECTION 1 (My Photos - Flickr)

Insert your Flickr ID, an Instagram search, or a dynamic or static collection of photos

SECTION 2 (My Blog - RSS)

Copy the RSS from your blog

SECTION 3 (My Music - Collection/Action Item)

Add an Action Item "MixRadio" and add the playlist IDs for your favorite bands and artists

SECTION 4 (About Me - HTML)

Describe yourself and your app in this main section
